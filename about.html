About This Project

The Purpose

This portfolio serves as a comprehensive look into my work within the Fundamentals of GIS. By integrating technical ArcGIS Pro proficiency with data-driven analysis, I have developed a series of maps that address complex spatial questions. From understanding the nuances of coordinate systems to performing vector analysis in urban environments, this project demonstrates the intersection of geography, data analytics, and visual communication.
